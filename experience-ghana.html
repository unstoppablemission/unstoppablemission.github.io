Upcoming Mission Trip Opportunities

If you would like to go along on a mission trip to Ghana in 2016 Sunnybrook Church will be offering two opportunities. The first trip will be June 1-10, 2016 and the second trip will be July 28 – August 8, 2016. During the trip to Ghana you will be exposed to each ministry from Orphan Care to Discipleship. Our goal is to provide an exposure trip where participants can begin to ask the question how does God want to use my gifts and talents on the global mission field. The cost for each trip is airline ticket ($1,500.00 – $2,000.00) + $750.00 which covers all in-country expenses such as lodging, transportation, and food. Each participant is responsible for raising their own support to attend. If you are interested please fill out the contact form below and Jake Sullivan or another member of the Sunnybrook mission staff will be in touch with you shortly after receiving all your information.

When you join us on mission in Ghana, Africa not only are you choosing to be a voice for the oppressed and the loving arms for the orphan but you are choosing to be a light of advocacy to your local church, in your neighborhoods, and in your work places. When we put our faith in Christ we have also made a proclamation to spend ourselves on behalf of the oppressed to be a light for Christ in the darkest places of this world.

If you want to join us in being that light and being that voice amongst the nations please contact Jake Sullivan at 515-291-1542 or via email jakes@sunnybrookchurch.org.
