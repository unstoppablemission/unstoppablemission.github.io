Upcoming Mission Trip Opportunities

If you would like to go along on a mission trip to Ghana in 2016, Sunnybrook Church will be offering two opportunities:

  1. June 1-10, 2016
  2. July 28 – August 8, 2016.

Our goal is to provide an exposure trip where participants can begin to ask the question, "How does God want to use my gifts and talents on the global mission field?" During the trip to Ghana you will be exposed to each ministry, from Orphan Care to Discipleship.

Cost

Each participant is responsible for raising their own support to attend and the cost for each trip ranges from $2,250 to $2,750:

  1. $1,500.00 – $2,000.00 - airline ticket
  2. $750.00 - all in-country expenses such as lodging, transportation, and food
