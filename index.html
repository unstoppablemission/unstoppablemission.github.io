ADVOCATE

ADVOCATE

We believe that as Christians we have a responsibility to be a voice for the oppressed, a place for the orphan, and relief for the widow.

AID

AID

Aid (Mercy) ministry meets immediate needs of the hungry, the distressed, and caring for the orphan & widow. It moves beyond merely sympathizing, and puts compassion into action by offering our time, talents, and resources.

DEVELOP

DEVELOP

If aid (mercy) ministry is giving a hungry person a fish, development is teaching that hungry person to fish. Development means working with individuals and communities toward a place of greater capability and sustainability.

LOCAL

SIOUX CITY, IA

Our vision is to create lasting relationships with students, families, and the community in hopes to provide long-term change on the West side of Sioux City.

REGIONAL

HAITI, CARIBBEAN

For 10 years Sunnybrook has been dedicated to meeting the needs of multiple communities within the country of Haiti, including medical, educational, physical, and sharing the gospel.

GLOBAL

GHANA, AFRICA

Join us on mission in Ghana, Africa and choose not only to be a voice for the oppressed and the loving arms for the orphan, but also to be a light of advocacy in your local church, neighborhood, and work place.
